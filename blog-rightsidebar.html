How to prevent vitamin B12 deficiency during winter?

In winter, vitamin B12 is key to avoiding fatigue and maintaining optimal concentration. Diversify your diet with lean meats, fish, eggs, and dairy products, or opt for supplements if necessary. Be careful not to exceed the recommended doses. An optimal balance of vitamin B12 ensures constant energy throughout the cold season.
